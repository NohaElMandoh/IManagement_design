ABOUT COMPANY

WE ARE HIT

At Interactive Management, we strive to maintain our leadership in the modern technology industry and align our goals with Saudi Vision 2030. We pride ourselves on delivering advanced solutions and exceptional customer service. As a Canadian-Saudi company based in Riyadh, we specialize in high-tech system integration with a particular focus on Digital Transformation. Our expertise includes Incident Management, Paperless Workflow Tech Solutions, Critical Communication, Automation, Command and Control, Remote Control, ATM, and C4ISR. Our proficiency in telecommunications engineering, IT, and industrial control engineering enables us to offer diverse services, including remote monitoring and tracking, low current systems, sensors, BMS applications, and unified communication systems. Our projects have evolved to specialize in designing and constructing command and control centers, crisis and disaster management rooms, monitoring rooms, meeting rooms, conference halls, and contact centers with smart applications and user-friendly interfaces. With over a decade of experience, we are committed to continuously developing our technologies and expertise to stay ahead of the Saudi market, leading the way in innovation and quality. Our dedication to executing specialized projects with precision and providing ongoing support in training, operation, and maintenance reflects our goal of serving and supporting our clients' investments with the latest technologies. We are always committed to continuous training and development to enhance our team's capabilities and ensure the highest level of service and quality. .
